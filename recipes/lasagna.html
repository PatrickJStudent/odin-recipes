Lasagna

A taste of Italy

Try this world famous recipe which Patrick one night came up with for the sake of a web development course, your taste buds will go on a culinary journey ranging from the unforgiving heat of Sicily to the more tempered and cultured Basque country of Spain (what?)

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of Canned Tomatoes
  • 1 set of lasagna plates
  • 300 grams of rinsed meat
  • 2 tbsps of Oregano
  • 8 cranks of black pepper
  • 2 tsp of Kosher salt
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 medium sized onion
  • 200 grams of mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Turn a heavy cast iron pan on to high heat and sear the meat in oil until it gets a nice and deep color.
  2. Cut the onion and garlic into small pieces and add to the meat. Turn heat to medium-high and sear until onions start sweating. Add canned tomatoes and boil until about half of the liquid remains
  3. Add the mixture to a pan and place the lasagna plates inbetween
  4. Shred the mozzarella and put it on top of everything.
  5. Put it into the oven on 250 degrees celcius for 40 minutes.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before eating.
