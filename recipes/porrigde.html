Porridge

A symphony of textures and subtle flavor in opulent homage to the humble grain

Velvety-smooth, artisanal porridge, delicately crafted from heirloom grains and slow-cooked to a flawless, creamy perfection. This elevated breakfast sensation is a symphony of nuanced textures and subtle flavors, an opulent homage to the humble grain.

Ingredients

  • 2 dl of grains
  • 3 dl of water
  • 1 pinch of salt (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in bowl
  2. Microwave for 3:30 minutes
